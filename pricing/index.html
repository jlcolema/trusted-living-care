Experience Eskaton Village Roseville

A resort-style community located in West Roseville near shops, restaurants, parks and medical services.

Our campus features a Lodge with assisted living, memory care and short-term stays, surrounded by an expansive neighborhood of several hundred private residences.

Comfort is the focus here, with personal services and amenities to make life easy and enjoyable.

This attractive community includes an active Village Center clubhouse with heated indoor swimming pool, courtyards, a popular bistro and state-of-the-art fitness room

– all great for socializing. Quiet park pathways and a well-stocked library offer space for study and contemplation.

Who We Are

You inspire us. Eskaton means “the dawning of a new day.” We find inspiration in becoming a part of your extended family.

Founded in 1968, Eskaton is now the largest nonprofit community-based organization serving seniors in the Greater Sacramento area. Our dedicated team members provide services and support for nearly 12,000 individuals annually who live in our communities or participate in our comprehensive Home Support Services. Eskaton’s difference lies in our broad array of choices and affordable options to engage and support people living in their own homes, as well as those who choose to live in one of our communities. Our positive reputation extends to our signature life-enriching programs, innovative health and wellness initiatives, professional staff training and development and generous philanthropy. These, along with our nonprofit status are Eskaton’s distinguishing factors.

The Nonprofit Difference: Nonprofit organizations help build community through volunteers and philanthropy, and are frequently the social responsibility conduit for other businesses. Profits are reinvested, not distributed to shareholders. They have transparent business practices and their leaders are accessible. Nonprofits generally have higher staffing levels and longer tenure among employees. Long-standing, established nonprofits often have resident assistance funds for those who outlive their financial resources. They have intrinsic value that even the best for-profit chains cannot offer; and during times of rapid and radical change like today, reputation and experience matter.

For you, your family, friends and neighbors … Eskaton is transforming the aging experience.
