WELCOME TO BELMONT VILLAGE SENIOR LIVING

LIVING OPTIONS

  • Independent Living
  • Assisted Living
  • Memory Care

    • At Belmont Village Westwood, you’ll find the perfect blend of comfort, care and active living. Our beautifully landscaped community is nestled in the heart of the Westwood area on Wilshire Boulevard, surrounded by premier dining, shopping and entertainment. We’re just a short drive from the UCLA Medical Center, Beverly Hills, Brentwood and the sunny beaches of Santa Monica. Step inside our vibrant community and discover the abundance of amenities, including an array of elegantly designed common areas for gatherings and a technology center to keep you virtually connected.

    Everything you need to thrive physically, socially and mentally is at your fingertips – a professionally managed fitness center with on-site therapy services, a licensed nurse and well-trained staff on-site 24/7, innovative, award-winning memory enrichment programs, and a vibrant social activity calendar. Enjoy restaurant-style dining and a chef-prepared meal with new friends at Josephine’s Kitchen, get pampered in our full-service salon, schedule complimentary transportation for personal excursions and appointments – the choice is yours. Our tenured management team and specially trained staff are here to provide quality care and just the right balance of service and support to suit any lifestyle.
